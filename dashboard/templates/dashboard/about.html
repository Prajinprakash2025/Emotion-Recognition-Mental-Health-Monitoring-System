About Us

What is MindMonitor AI?

A real-time emotion recognition system built with deep learning to help individuals understand and track their mental health through facial expression analysis.

Our Mission

Making Mental Health Visible

Mental health is often invisible. MindMonitor AI bridges that gap by using computer vision and AI to surface emotional patterns that people may not even notice themselves.

By tracking emotions over time, users gain actionable insights into their stress levels, mood trends, and overall emotional wellbeing — all from the privacy of their own device.
