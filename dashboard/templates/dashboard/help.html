Everything you need to know about using MindMonitor AI.

How It Works

MindMonitor AI uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on the FER-2013 dataset to analyze facial expressions in real-time. The pipeline is:

Face Detection

OpenCV Haar Cascades locate faces in each camera frame.

Preprocessing

Faces are cropped and resized to 48×48 pixels for the model.

AI Inference

The CNN predicts probabilities across 7 emotion classes every 0.3s.

Visualisation

Results are overlaid on the live feed and logged to your history.

Reading the Dashboard

Stress Level

Calculated from frequency of negative emotions (Anger, Fear, Disgust, Sadness). High levels trigger alerts.

Dominant Mood

The emotion with the highest cumulative confidence score over the current session.

Trend Chart

Shows stress and happiness levels over your last 30 logged data points.

Session Timer

Tracks how long the current monitoring session has been active.

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is our priority. Video data is processed locally in memory and is never stored or uploaded. Only the resulting emotion label and timestamp are saved to your personal history for progress tracking. You can view and manage your history at any time from the Reports page.
